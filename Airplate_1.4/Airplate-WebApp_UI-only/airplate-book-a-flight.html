We hope to bring people from all over the world, places and diverse cultures closer together. Serving our community and planet is at the heart of everything we do, and we look forward to sharing our exciting sustainability initiatives with you.

We offer you plenty of opportunities to personalise your experience with us by pre-ordering your meals ahead of your flight and inform cabin crew members of your delicate needs to ensure you have a safe and comfortable journey onboard.

Airplate exists today because of you. This is why we want to make every trip you take with us a great experience. Our entire team is here to provide an exceptional service just for you – from our signature meals to ensuring that you have an exquisite meal onboard prepared by our world class culinary teams. As an organisation with a mission goal of promoting sustainability, we have a responsibility to help create a sustainable future for our planet, and we’re on a mission to be carbon net zero by 2050.

In a world of overproduction of food and malnourished children, we hope to minimise food wastage starting with the airline industry. We hope that you will help us achieve this end goal and to create a sustainable future for the next generation. So think twice before you leave food on your meal tray.
